Our Mission

🚀 Echelon Dev Society (EDS), the Hackathon Club of CDGI, is your launchpad for turning ideas into impactful projects and becoming part of a community that thrives on innovation.

💼 Whether you're a coder, a creator, or someone ready to lead, EDS is the place to grow, innovate, and stand out.

What We Do

  • Weekly coding workshops and tech talks
  • Hackathons and project competitions
  • Collaborative open-source projects
  • Networking events with industry professionals
